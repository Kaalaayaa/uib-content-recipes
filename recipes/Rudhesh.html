Tutti Frutti cake

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup 190 grams curd / yogurt
  • ¾ cup 170 grams sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup 100 grams oil
  • <1½ cup 260 grams maida / plain flour / refined flour / all purpose flour/li>
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ cup water
  • ¼ cup 60 grams tutti frutti

Preparation

  1. firstly, in a large mixing bowl take ¾ cup curd, ¾ cup sugar, ½ cup oil and 1 tsp vanilla extract.
  2. mix well with the help of whisk.
  3. further sieve 1½ cup maida, ¼ tsp baking soda and 1 tsp baking powder.
  4. mix well using cut and fold method.
  5. further add ¼ cup water and mix well forming thick flowing consistency batter.
  6. add in ¼ cup of tutti frutti and give a good mix.
  7. further, transfer the cake batter into the cake mould or bread mould (width: 12 cm, height: 6 cm, length: 26 cm). make sure to grease the mould with butter to avoid sticking and line butter paper at bottom of tray.
  8. level up the batter and pat the tray twice to remove the air incorporated into the batter.
  9. place the cake tray into preheated oven. bake the cake at 180 degree celsius or 356 degree fahrenheit for 30-40 minutes.
  10. finally, serve the tutti frutti cake or store in airtight container.
