André's Negrinhos

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of condensed milk (794 g)
  • 150 g of butter
  • 3 full soup spoons of chocolate powder
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Preparation

  1. Melt 100 g of butter in a pot.
  2. Add chocolate powder on the butter and mix it well (spoons should be full, with a mountain on top).
  3. Add condensed milk and turn the heat up mix it well specially when it starts to boil.
  4. When the consistency is good, stop the fire (The consistency is good when you pass a spoon on the bottom of the pot and you can see the bottom for a fraction of a second).
  5. Wait for the dough to cool down.
  6. Spread butter on your hands and make balls with the dough.
  7. Cover the balls with sprinkles.
